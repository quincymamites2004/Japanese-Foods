Takoyaki


      As far as Japanese street vendor foods are concerned, few are more notorious than Takoyaki. Also known as ‘octopus balls’ or ‘octopus dumplings’, this delicacy is cooked using a special hot plate with rows of half-spherical molds. Each of the molds is filled with a savory batter mixture before a bite-size piece of Takoyaki octopus meat is inserted into the middle.
